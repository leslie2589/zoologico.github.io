Members do not need to book in advance.

Visitors with disabilities may admit one accompanying carer free of charge when they pay for a full adult or child ticket. Please bring relevant proof of entitlement such as a blue badge, or a Personal Independence Payments letter (issued by the DWP benefit office). Our team will ask to see this then you enter the zoo at our admission lanes.

A child ticket is for a person aged from 3 to 17 years old inclusive.

No child under the age of 14 years will be admitted to the Zoo unless they are accompanied by an adult who is over the age of 18 years.

For all of the latest safety information related to your visit to the zoo please take a look at our Visitor Charter.

MEET EXPERIENCES

Each person taking part in an Experience must complete a Risk Acknowledgement and Disclaimer form to confirm their booking which will be included in your email confirmation. Please complete this form when completing your online booking. Should the person participating in the experience have any Visual/Hearing/Mobility Impairment or have any Learning Difficulties/Disabilities please call us on 01244 380280.

DINE EXPERIENCES

If you have any special dietary requirements or allegeries please ensure you complete the form that will be included in your confirmation email. Please complete this form when completing your online booking. For any further information please call us on 01244 380280.
